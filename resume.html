My Journey & How It Applies

My path has been unconventional but intentional, providing me with a unique blend of analytical, legal, and entrepreneurial skills directly applicable to the Strategy Associate role.

Relevance: This embedded a deep, intuitive understanding of business operations, client management, and the relentless work ethic required to succeed—core traits for any strategy role.

Relevance: This demonstrates resilience and a steep learning curve. The combination of accounting (the language of business) and programming (the language of logic and systems) provides a powerful analytical foundation.

Legal & Analytical Acumen

Inspired by defending my family's business in a $9,999 lawsuit at age 18, I pursued a JD to master high-stakes communication and argumentation. This experience, combined with a 740 GMAT score, honed my ability to perform quick, accurate, back-of-the-napkin analysis under pressure.

Relevance: Law school provides a rigorous framework for structured thinking and persuasive communication—essential for creating compelling memos and strategic recommendations.
