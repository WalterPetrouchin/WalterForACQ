My Goals & Aspirations

I am driven by a desire for continuous growth and impact. Here is a look at what I aim to achieve, both in the near future with your team and in my long-term career.

My First 90 Days

Assuming I earn the role, here is how I plan to approach my first three months to create immediate value.

First 30 Days

Deep Immersion & Immediate Contribution

  • Self-Onboard Proactively: Take the initiative to onboard myself by absorbing all available documentation, memos, and frameworks, while eagerly welcoming guidance from leadership to accelerate my integration.
  • Connect with the Advisory Team: Meet with every member of the advisory group to understand their roles, learn from their experience, and ask for their advice on how to succeed at ACQ.
  • Own the "Boring Work": Accelerate the team by taking meticulous notes in meetings, preparing first drafts of memos and decks, and organizing raw information into structured, usable formats.
  • Drive the Deliverable Process: Add immediate value by refining existing deliverables for clarity, conducting market research to support current projects, and representing the ACQ brand professionally in all interactions.
  • Support Data Analysis: Join client discovery calls to listen and synthesize key problems, and assist in gathering and analyzing data to support the team's strategic insights.
First 60 Days

Expanding Impact & Taking Ownership

  • Extend Internal Outreach: Connect with key members of other departments (Marketing, Sales, Tech) to build a holistic understanding of the business and foster cross-functional relationships.
  • Own a Deliverable: Transition from preparing first drafts to taking full ownership of a smaller, well-defined client deliverable from initial analysis to final refinement.
  • Identify a Pain Point: Based on observations, identify one internal process or client-facing framework that could be improved and write a "shitty first draft" of a memo proposing a solution.
  • Master Core Frameworks: Demonstrate a strong working knowledge of Acquisition.com's primary consulting frameworks by actively applying them in client work and internal discussions.
First 90 Days

Driving Results & Autonomous Operation

  • Deliver a Tangible Result: Move from identifying a pain point to executing the solution. My goal is to deliver a specific, measurable improvement to the process or framework I identified.
  • Contribute a Strategic Insight: Leverage pattern recognition across clients to identify an overarching theme or trend, and present this strategic insight to the team in a well-researched memo.
  • Operate Autonomously: Demonstrate the ability to manage my projects and priorities with minimal oversight, consistently delivering high-quality results on or ahead of schedule.
  • Become a Go-To Resource: Through my contributions and understanding of the business, aim to become a reliable and trusted resource for the advisory team on at least one key framework or process.

Long-Term Aspirations

Beyond the first 90 days, my ambitions are focused on deep expertise, leadership, and a well-rounded life.

Master the Craft: My primary goal is to master the scalable business frameworks and playbooks at the heart of Acquisition.com. I want to develop the ability to diagnose business constraints and provide high-leverage solutions on the spot, just as Alex does in the Hormozi Hotline series.

Grow with ACQ: I am applying to ACQ with the intention of building my career here. I am eager to grow tremendously within the Strategy Associate role and earn my way into senior leadership, contributing to the company's long-term success.

Build a Family: Outside of my professional ambitions, a core part of my life's vision is to get married and raise a family of 3-5 kids, building a strong and loving home.

Maintain Health & Vitality: I am blessed with excellent health, and I am committed to maintaining it. Staying in peak physical and mental condition is crucial for performing at a high level in all areas of my life.
